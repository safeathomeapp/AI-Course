## Rejected suggestions
1. Expand this module into a deep multi-tool comparison and full prompt-iteration playbook (`T1M04-08`): rejected as out-of-scope for foundation level.
2. Reframe module away from predictability toward open-ended creativity (`T1M04-09`): rejected as a core-direction change not supported by broad evidence.

## Notes on what should not be changed in the module
1. Do not remove the five-part RCTCO core model.
2. Do not remove the failure-mode mapping per element; it is the strongest retention and transfer mechanism.
3. Do not remove the real prompt before/after test exercise format.
4. Do not remove the prompt library artefact or Review Notes field.
5. Do not dilute the governance continuity message that human review retains final quality ownership.
6. Do not increase technical density at the expense of current accessibility gains.
