# 11. Missing Content

Module is well-scoped.

What would strengthen it for sales and operations leaders:

1. **Sales or revenue operations demonstration.** Pipeline analysis, proposal drafting, competitive intelligence, account research — all major AI use cases with clear high/medium/low risk distribution.

2. **Team enforcement mechanisms.** How do you operationalize the matrix across a team? What workflow gates or review checkpoints make verification protocols durable rather than aspirational? Governance at a VP level is a team management challenge, not just a personal practice challenge.

3. **Escalation design.** When an AI output uncertainty triggers escalation, where does it go? Who is the escalation owner? The matrix defines review levels but doesn't address escalation paths for edge cases.
