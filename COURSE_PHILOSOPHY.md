# COURSE_PHILOSOPHY.md

## Core Philosophy

Artificial Intelligence is not an authority.
It is a tool.

Professionals must understand how it behaves before using it in real work.

AI drafts → Humans review → Humans decide

---

## Central Model

Prompt → Context → Generation → Human Review → Decision

AI proposes.
Humans decide.

---

## Why This Course Exists

Most AI courses are either:

1. superficial prompt tricks
2. deep machine learning theory

Professionals need practical understanding between these extremes.

---

## Learning Outcomes

Students should be able to:

- use AI safely in professional work
- design repeatable AI workflows
- evaluate reliability and risk
- integrate AI into real decision processes
