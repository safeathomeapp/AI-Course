# AI_COURSE_ROADMAP.md

## Project Title

An AI's Guide for Humans: Understanding and Harnessing Artificial
Intelligence

## Project Vision

Artificial Intelligence is becoming a universal professional tool. Like
a hammer, scalpel, or microscope, its effectiveness depends on the skill
of the person using it.

This course teaches learners how to deliberately and professionally use
AI systems to enhance their work, thinking, and decision‑making.

The course focuses on: - understanding how AI behaves - structuring
instructions to produce reliable outputs - integrating AI into real
workflows - designing AI‑assisted systems

## Core Philosophy

AI is a tool, not an authority. Reliable outputs come from structured
thinking and workflow design. Principles outlast tools. AI amplifies
capable professionals.

## Course Architecture

### Tier 1 --- Foundations of AI

Duration: \~20 hours

Purpose: Understand AI behaviour and gain control over outputs.

Outcomes: - structured prompting - understanding AI limitations - simple
workflow design - AI-assisted writing and research

Portfolio Artefacts: - personal prompt library - structured prompt
templates - AI-assisted research report

### Tier 2 --- Applied AI Workflows

Duration: 20--30 hours

Purpose: Turn AI use into repeatable workflows.

Outcomes: - prompt chains - workflow decomposition - AI-assisted
analysis - operational documentation

Portfolio Artefacts: - AI workflow documentation - AI-enhanced SOP -
analytical framework using AI

### Tier 3 --- AI Systems and Architecture

Duration: 30--40 hours

Purpose: Design AI‑enabled tools and systems.

Outcomes: - AI assistants - retrieval systems - automation pipelines -
workflow architecture

Portfolio Artefacts: - AI assistant architecture - knowledge retrieval
system concept - AI tool prototype
