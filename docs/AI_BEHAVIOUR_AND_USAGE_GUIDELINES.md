# AI_BEHAVIOUR_AND_USAGE_GUIDELINES.md

## Purpose

Defines how AI should be approached throughout this course.

## Principles

### AI Is Not an Authority

AI produces probabilistic responses. Humans must verify and evaluate
outputs.

### Use Structured Instructions

Professional AI use requires: - role definition - context - task
definition - output constraints

### Verification Is Required

Always: - cross‑check important facts - question assumptions - verify
outputs

### Use AI Within Workflows

Break tasks into stages: research → summarise → analyse → produce →
review

### Humans Remain Accountable

AI assists but responsibility always remains with the user.

### Avoid Blind Automation

Understand processes before automating them.

### Focus on Systems, Not Tricks

The durable skill is workflow design and structured thinking.
