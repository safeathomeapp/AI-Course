# COURSE_CASE_STUDIES.md

## Purpose

Defines recurring scenarios used throughout the course to demonstrate
concepts.

Using consistent case studies improves learning because students see the
same problems solved with increasingly advanced techniques.

## Case Study 1 --- Finance & Bookkeeping

Scenario: A small business must review monthly financial data.

AI Tasks: - summarise financial performance - detect anomalies -
generate a management report

## Case Study 2 --- Software Development

Scenario: A developer must understand and maintain an unfamiliar
codebase.

AI Tasks: - explain code - generate documentation - identify potential
bugs

## Case Study 3 --- Product Management

Scenario: A product team is deciding whether to launch a new feature.

AI Tasks: - analyse user feedback - identify risks - produce a product
brief

## Case Study 4 --- Marketing & Content

Scenario: A company wants to launch a new marketing campaign.

AI Tasks: - audience analysis - campaign concept generation - content
planning

## Case Study 5 --- Operations & Administration

Scenario: An operations manager needs to document internal processes.

AI Tasks: - convert meeting notes to SOPs - produce workflow diagrams -
summarise operational issues

## Case Study 6 --- Customer Support

Scenario: A support team receives large volumes of customer requests.

AI Tasks: - classify tickets - generate response drafts - identify
common product issues
